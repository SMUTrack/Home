Our members

We are a friendly bunch of atheletes! Anyone is welcomed to join us, even if you have no track and field background.

We take part in annual inter-varisty competitions such as the SuNiG Road Race and the IVP Track and Field Championship, and some of our atheletes have even competed in prestigious events such as the ASEAN University Games!

For further inquires, feel free to contact us through tracknfield@sa.smu.edu.sg
